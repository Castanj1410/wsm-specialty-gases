What We Manufacture

Calibration Gas for Gas Detectors & More

Every calibration gas mixture manufactured, analyzed, and certified at our ISO-accredited laboratory in Miami. Full COA with every cylinder.

🎯

4-Gas & Multi-Gas Mix

CO / H₂S / LEL / O₂ — the industry standard for portable gas detector calibration. Also 3-gas, 5-gas, and custom multi-gas blends.

⚡

Bump Test & Span Gas

Quick-response bump test gas for daily detector verification. Span calibration gas for full detector calibration per manufacturer specs.

EPA Protocol Gas (PGVP)

EPA Protocol gases certified under the Protocol Gas Verification Program. NIST-traceable for CEMS and emissions compliance (40 CFR Part 72).

Single Gas Standards

H₂S, CO, SO₂, NO, NO₂, HCl, Cl₂, NH₃, LEL (CH₄, C₃H₈, C₄H₁₀), O₂ depletion — any single gas at any concentration.

LEL calibration blends: methane, propane, pentane, isobutane. FID fuel gas (H₂/He, H₂/N₂). Natural gas simulation blends for utilities.
