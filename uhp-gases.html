What is UHP argon used for?

+
UHP argon (99.999%) is widely used in semiconductor manufacturing for chamber purging and wafer processing, in welding as a high-purity shielding gas, in analytical laboratories for ICP and mass spectrometry, and in metal heat treatment. Demand for UHP argon is growing rapidly driven by semiconductor expansion.

Which carrier gas is best for gas chromatography?

+
UHP helium is the most commonly used gas chromatography carrier gas due to its low molecular weight, chemical inertness, and optimal separation efficiency. UHP hydrogen is an alternative that offers faster analysis times. UHP nitrogen is used for cost-sensitive applications. WSM supplies all three in GC-compatible grades with full COA.
