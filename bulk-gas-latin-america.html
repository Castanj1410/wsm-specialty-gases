What bulk cryogenic products does WSM supply to Latin America?

+
WSM supplies bulk liquid nitrogen (LIN), liquid oxygen (LOX), liquid argon (LAR), liquid carbon dioxide (CO₂), and helium to Latin America and the Caribbean via ISO tank containers. All products are sourced and coordinated from our Miami logistics hub.

Which countries does WSM deliver bulk gas to?

+
WSM delivers bulk cryogenic products to Venezuela, Colombia, Mexico, Puerto Rico, Dominican Republic, Panama, Costa Rica, Guatemala, Argentina, Chile, Ecuador, Peru, Brazil, Trinidad & Tobago, Jamaica, Curaçao, Aruba, and other Caribbean and Latin American markets. Contact us for availability in your specific region.

How does WSM handle international bulk gas logistics?

+
WSM manages end-to-end logistics: ISO tank container loading, ocean freight booking and vessel tracking, customs clearance and import documentation, inland transport, and last-mile delivery to your facility. All shipments are fully HAZMAT compliant (49 CFR, IMDG, IATA) with emergency response plans and MSDS documentation.

Does WSM offer cryogenic repair services?

+
Yes. WSM provides complete cryogenic equipment repair including vacuum testing, valve replacement, pressure system restoration, and insulation repair. We also repair ISO tank containers and tank trailers — structural, mechanical, and safety system repairs to return equipment to full service.

What ISO tank certification services does WSM provide?

+
WSM performs ISO tank periodic inspections and re-certifications: the 2.5 year intermediate inspection and the 5 year periodic inspection. Both include DOT/IMO compliance verification and full documentation. Keeping your ISO tanks certified is essential for continued international transport operations.

Why is Miami the ideal hub for bulk gas export to Latin America?

+
Miami provides the shortest and most frequent ocean freight routes to Latin America and the Caribbean. Direct shipping lines connect to all major ports in the region. Combined with Miami's established HAZMAT logistics infrastructure, customs brokerage services, and WSM's local expertise, it's the most efficient gateway for bulk cryogenic delivery to the Americas.
